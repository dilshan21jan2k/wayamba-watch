About PublicAlert LK

PublicAlert LK is a comprehensive public safety platform designed to enhance community resilience and emergency preparedness across Sri Lanka. Our system enables real-time reporting and tracking of various incidents including natural disasters, infrastructure issues, and public safety concerns.

By leveraging modern web technologies and community participation, we aim to create a safer, more informed society where citizens can actively contribute to public safety and emergency response efforts.
